The Gulf Cooperation Council market is one of the fastest-growing health and wellness regions globally, driven by government wellness initiatives and a young, digitally-connected consumer base. Omar Al-Mansoori, a Middle East Health Importer based in Dubai, UAE, is bringing HLK products into this market.

Omar visit combined business with relationship-building - a crucial element in Middle Eastern business culture. After a full tour of the nasal strip and mouth tape production lines, the HLK team hosted him for an evening dinner where the real partnership took shape. The production capacity that impressed Omar - 5,000 square meters of automated manufacturing space capable of scaling from small-batch trial runs to full-container orders - is exactly what Gulf distributors need to test new product categories without committing to excessive inventory.

His initial order covers nasal strips and sleep mouth tape, with face tape and sports kinesiology tape planned for a follow-up order once the first container clears Dubai Customs.

> Great dinner and in-depth business discussion with HLK Tape team. We toured the factory yesterday and were impressed by production capacity. We aim to introduce HLK nasal strips and sleep mouth tape into Gulf market.

**Omar Al-Mansoori - Middle East Health Importer - Dubai, UAE**
