Standard nasal strips fail in tropical climates. Since 2023, Lim Wei Jie has worked with HLK R&D to develop a sweat-resistant adhesive formula for over 85 percent humidity and over 32 degrees Celsius. The formula took three iterations to perfect - increasing zinc oxide content for grip without skin irritation. AsiaSport now carries HLK mouth tape, kinesiology tape, and sleep patches across Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, and the Philippines. A recent reformulation increased adhesive hold time by an additional 15 percent in humidity chamber testing.
