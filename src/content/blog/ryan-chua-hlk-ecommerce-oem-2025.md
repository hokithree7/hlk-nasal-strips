Selling on Shopee and Lazada is fundamentally different from traditional wholesale distribution. The MOQ must be flexible enough to test new SKUs. Sample delivery must be fast enough to get product photos and listings live before competitors. Packaging must be compliant with each platform requirements - and look good in a 1000 by 1000 pixel product image on a smartphone screen.

Ryan Chua, a Cross-border E-commerce Seller and Shopee/Lazada Top Seller based in Singapore, knows every detail of this game. When he visited HLK, he was not looking for a traditional OEM supplier - he was looking for a manufacturing partner who understood e-commerce velocity.

Ryan visit focused on three things that matter to online sellers: flexible MOQ to test the market and scale what works, fast sample delivery to get product photos and listings up in days not weeks, and platform-compliant packaging - barcodes, ingredient labels, tamper-evident seals that meet Shopee and Lazada requirements. His first order covered mouth tape and nasal strips for the Singapore and Malaysia markets. Within 60 days of launch, both SKUs hit Top Seller status on his Shopee storefront.

> HLK Tape understands Southeast Asian e-commerce market requirements very well. Flexible MOQ, fast sample delivery and compliant packaging solutions can perfectly match our online retail business. Looking forward to long-term strategic partnership.

**Ryan Chua - Cross-border E-commerce Seller - Shopee/Lazada Top Seller, Singapore**
