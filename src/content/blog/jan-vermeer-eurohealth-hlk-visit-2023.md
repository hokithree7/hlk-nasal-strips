European medical device distribution is one of the hardest markets to enter. Every certification must be genuine. Every document must be traceable. Every communication must be precise.

Jan Vermeer, CEO and Founder of EuroHealth Supply B.V. (Netherlands), visited HLK facility with a singular focus: verifying that our CE MDR and ISO 13485 certifications were not just certificates on a wall, but embedded in every process on the production floor.

He spent three hours on the factory floor, cross-checking batch records against our certification documentation. He traced a single batch of mouth tape from raw material receipt to finished packaging, checking the paper trail at each station. At the end of the visit, he told our team: The documentation matches the reality. That is rarer than you think.

> As a European distributor, regulatory compliance is everything for us. HLK CE MDR and ISO 13485 certifications are genuine and well-documented. Their team speaks fluent English which makes communication seamless.

**Jan Vermeer - CEO and Founder - EuroHealth Supply B.V., Netherlands**
