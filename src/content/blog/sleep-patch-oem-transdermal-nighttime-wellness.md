## Why Sleep Patches Are the Next Shelf Essential

The transdermal patch market is projected to grow at over 7% annually through 2030. Three consumer behaviors are accelerating sleep patch adoption specifically:

- **Melatonin fatigue**: oral melatonin sales are plateauing as consumers seek alternatives with fewer digestive side effects and more consistent delivery
- **Ingredient stacking**: patches can combine multiple sleep botanicals (valerian, passionflower, lavender, hops, chamomile) in a single sustained-release format that pills cannot match
- **Overnight wellness routine**: patches fit seamlessly into the bedtime mouth-tape / nasal-strip / sleep-patch habit stack that health-conscious consumers are adopting

Unlike oral supplements, transdermal delivery bypasses the digestive system entirely, providing higher bioavailability and steady absorption throughout the night.
