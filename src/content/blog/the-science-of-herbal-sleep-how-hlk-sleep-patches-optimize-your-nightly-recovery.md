## The Transdermal Advantage: How Sleep Patches Work

One of the most effective ways to deliver herbal ingredients is through the skin, a method known as transdermal delivery. HLK Sleep Patches utilize this advanced technology to provide a steady, controlled release of active ingredients throughout the night.

Unlike oral supplements, which must pass through the digestive system and the liver (the "first-pass effect"), transdermal patches deliver ingredients directly into the bloodstream through the skin layers. This ensures:
1.  **Higher Bioavailability**: More of the active herbal compounds reach your system.

3.  **No Digestive Irritation**: Ideal for those with sensitive stomachs who cannot tolerate oral supplements.
