## The Nasal Valve: The Bottleneck of Human Respiration
To understand how a nasal strip works, one must understand the anatomy of the nose. The narrowest part of the nasal airway is the internal nasal valve. When you inhale deeply, the negative pressure can cause these narrow passages to collapse, restricting airflow. This is where mechanical engineering meets human biology. A high-performance nasal strip is designed to provide "extranasal" support, counteracting this collapse and widening the airway.

## The Three Pillars of Nasal Strip Engineering
A premium HLK nasal strip is composed of three critical components, each engineered for a specific function:

### 1. The High-Tensile Spring Bands
The "engine" of the nasal strip is the embedded polyester or plastic bands. These aren't just rigid pieces of material; they are calibrated springs. When the strip is applied to the bridge of the nose, these bands strive to return to their original flat shape. This tension creates an upward lift on the lateral walls of the nose, physically pulling the nasal passages open. HLK?? 20 years of manufacturing experience allow us to calibrate this tension perfectly: strong enough to provide a 30% increase in airflow, yet gentle enough to be comfortable for 8+ hours of sleep.

### 2. Medical-Grade Adhesion Technology
A spring is useless if it doesn't stay attached. The adhesive layer is the most complex part of the engineering process. It must be strong enough to resist the recoil force of the spring bands and the natural oils of the skin, yet gentle enough to be removed without causing irritation or "skin stripping." At HLK, we use medical-grade, hypoallergenic acrylic adhesives that are ISO 13485 certified. This ensures that global distributors can offer a product that is safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

### 3. The Structural Carrier Fabric
The outer layer (the carrier) provides the structural integrity and breathability. Whether it is a classic tan fabric or a transparent film, the material must be flexible enough to contour to the unique shape of every nose while remaining breathable to prevent moisture build-up.
