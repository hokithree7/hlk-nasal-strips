Pharmacie Centrale, a French pharmacy chain, does not accept incomplete documentation. Every product on their shelf requires MSDS (Material Safety Data Sheet), CoC (Certificate of Conformity), batch-level traceability, and packaging that meets French retail standards.

Olivier Dubois, Category Buyer at Pharmacie Centrale (France), visited HLK to verify that we could meet those standards - not just for one order, but consistently, across seasons, even during peak production periods. He reviewed our entire documentation package for three product SKUs. He checked lot numbers against SGS test reports. He verified that our MSDS sheets matched the exact adhesive formulation used in the batch he sampled. He found zero omissions.

He also noted something that matters enormously to pharmacy buyers: our lead times remained stable during the Q4 peak season - a period when many suppliers extend delivery windows by two to three weeks. HLK lead times did not slip. Pharmacie Centrale now carries HLK nasal strips in over 200 pharmacy locations across France.

> We source medical-grade nasal strips for our pharmacy chain across France. HLK product documentation (MSDS, CoC) is always complete and accurate. Their lead times are reliable even during peak season. Very professional partner.

**Olivier Dubois - Category Buyer - Pharmacie Centrale, France**
