In 2025, Luca Rossi arrived at HLK factory with a checklist, a notebook, and Italian expectations. He inspected every station on the mouth tape and nasal strip production lines - adhesive strength, skin irritation testing, packaging labeling, traceability. Every check passed. His attention to the automated pouch sealing line (2,000 units per shift) reflected Italian retail packaging standards. HLK earned the EU Top-Seller Supplier qualification under the EU Picks Program without exceptions.

Stable lead time and flexible OEM customization make HLK our most reliable Chinese manufacturer for European market.
