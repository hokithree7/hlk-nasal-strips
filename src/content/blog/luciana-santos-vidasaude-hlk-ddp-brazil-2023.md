South American buyers face a logistics reality that European and North American distributors rarely encounter: shipping costs can erase margins. When Luciana Santos, Import Director at VidaSaude Brasil Ltda., first contacted HLK, her priority was not just product quality - it was landed cost.

Luciana imports nasal strips in bulk for the Brazilian market, where price sensitivity is high and retail competition is fierce. She needed a supplier who could deliver CE-certified quality at wholesale pricing that still made sense after freight, customs, and local taxes.

Our DDP logistics solution to Sao Paulo was the deciding factor. By consolidating her nasal strip orders with other South American-bound shipments, we reduced her per-unit landed cost by nearly 20 percent compared to her previous supplier FOB terms. She now places quarterly orders covering nasal strips, mouth tape, and sleep patches.
