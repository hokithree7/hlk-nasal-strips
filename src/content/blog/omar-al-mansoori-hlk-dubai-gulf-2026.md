In 2026, Omar Al-Mansoori visited HLK factory in Quanzhou to bring nasal strips and mouth tape into the Gulf Cooperation Council market - one of the fastest-growing health and wellness regions worldwide. After touring the 5,000-square-meter production facility, the HLK team hosted him for an evening dinner where the strategic partnership took shape. His initial order covers nasal strips and sleep mouth tape, with face tape and kinesiology tape planned for a follow-up container.

> Great dinner and in-depth business discussion with HLK Tape team. We toured the factory yesterday and were impressed by production capacity. We aim to introduce HLK nasal strips and sleep mouth tape into Gulf market.

**Omar Al-Mansoori - Middle East Health Importer - Dubai, UAE**
