Italian buyers are famous for inspecting everything. When Luca Rossi, Procurement Director at a European Sleep Brand (Italy), arrived at HLK factory, he came with a checklist, a notebook, and the expectation that he would find gaps. He left with a signed contract.

Luca inspected every station on the mouth tape and nasal strip production lines. He cross-checked product samples against EU market requirements for adhesive strength, skin irritation, packaging labeling, and traceability. Every check passed. He paid particular attention to the automated packaging line - the pouch filling and sealing station that processes 2,000 units per shift - because Italian retail packaging standards are among the strictest in Europe.

The EU Top-Seller Supplier qualification he references - obtained under the EU Picks Program - is one of the most rigorous supplier vetting processes for European importers. HLK passed it without exceptions.

> It is a fantastic visit to HLK Tape factory. We inspected the whole production line of mouth tape and nasal strips. Product quality meets EU standards perfectly. Stable lead time and flexible OEM customization make HLK our most reliable Chinese manufacturer for European market. We are glad HLK Tape has obtained the EU Top-Seller Supplier qualification under EU Picks Program.

**Luca Rossi - Procurement Director - European Sleep Brand, Italy**
