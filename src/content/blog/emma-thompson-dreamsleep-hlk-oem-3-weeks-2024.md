Speed-to-market matters more than almost anything for a brand owner launching a new sleep wellness product. Emma Thompson, Brand Owner of DreamSleep Co. (Australia), discovered this when she approached HLK with an idea, not a finished specification.

Emma wanted to launch her own branded line of sleep strips. She had a design concept, a rough packaging sketch, and a launch date - but no manufacturing partner. Her previous experiences with other suppliers had taught her that custom OEM often meant we will get back to you in six weeks.

Three weeks. That is the time from Emma first email to the first production run. Our OEM workflow - design confirmation, material selection, die-cut mold preparation, packaging proof, small-batch trial, production - completed in 21 days. The custom packaging she praised - matte-finish resealable pouches with gold foil stamping - has become a key driver of her brand shelf appeal in Australian pharmacies.

> Launched our own brand of sleep strips with HLK OEM service. From design concept to first production run took just 3 weeks. The custom packaging looks premium and our customers love it. Best supplier we have worked with.

**Emma Thompson - Brand Owner - DreamSleep Co., Australia**
