## Why Sports Tape Deserves a Spot in Your Product Line

Three buyer behaviors drive sustained demand for athletic tape:

- **Combat sports growth**: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, judo, wrestling, and MMA practitioners tape their fingers before every training session. It is not optional, it is gear.
- **CrossFit and functional fitness**: Callus protection, wrist support, and thumb wrapping are daily routines in box gyms worldwide.
- **Injury prevention and recovery**: Physiotherapists and sports medicine clinics use cohesive bandages for compression, support, and post-injury recovery. Repeat purchases are guaranteed.

Unlike trendy wellness products that spike and fade, athletes buy finger tape every month. It is a consumable commodity with a permanent user base.
