In 2023, Jan Vermeer, CEO and Founder of EuroHealth Supply B.V., flew from the Netherlands to Quanzhou with a singular objective: verify that HLK CE MDR and ISO 13485 certifications were embedded in every process, not just framed on a wall. He spent three hours cross-checking batch records against certification documentation, tracing a single batch from raw material receipt to finished packaging. At the end, he said: The documentation matches the reality - that is rarer than you think.

> As a European distributor, regulatory compliance is everything for us. HLK CE MDR and ISO 13485 certifications are genuine and well-documented. Their team speaks fluent English which makes communication seamless.

**Jan Vermeer - CEO and Founder - EuroHealth Supply B.V., Netherlands**
