## The Five Product Lines That Define Our Factory

The bone-shape 66x19mm design uses embedded high-tensile spring bands calibrated to reduce nasal airway resistance by approximately 30 percent. Our sweat-resistant hybrid adhesive holds through 8-plus hours in over 85 percent humidity ??a formula developed specifically for tropical and athletic markets.

### 3. Face Tape
Multi-color kinesiology-style elastic cotton strips with 60 percent stretch for forehead, cheek-lift, and jawline taping routines. Available in blue, pink, orange, yellow, purple, teal, and magenta. 30 strips per Custom Logo OEM pouch. The fastest-growing subcategory in the sleep-and-beauty crossover market.
