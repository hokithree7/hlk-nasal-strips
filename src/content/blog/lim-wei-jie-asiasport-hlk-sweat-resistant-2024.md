Standard nasal strips fail in tropical climates. The adhesive that holds through a cool European morning will peel off during a humid Singapore afternoon run. Lim Wei Jie, Operations Director at AsiaSport Distribution (Singapore), knew this before he even contacted HLK - because he had tested competitors products and watched them fail.

Since 2023, HLK R&D team has worked directly with Lim specifications to develop a sweat-resistant adhesive formula optimized for over 85 percent humidity and over 32 degrees Celsius ambient temperature. The formula increases zinc oxide content to boost grip without skin irritation - a delicate balance that took three iterations to perfect.

The partnership has grown beyond nasal strips. AsiaSport Distribution now carries HLK mouth tape, sports kinesiology tape, and sleep patches across Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, and the Philippines. Lim feedback loop with our R&D team continues to drive product improvements - including a recent reformulation that increased adhesive hold time by an additional 15 percent in humidity chamber testing.

> HLK has been supplying us with sports performance strips for the APAC region since 2023. The sweat-resistant adhesive formula they developed for tropical climates works exceptionally well. Great R&D support from their team.

**Lim Wei Jie - Operations Director - AsiaSport Distribution, Singapore**
