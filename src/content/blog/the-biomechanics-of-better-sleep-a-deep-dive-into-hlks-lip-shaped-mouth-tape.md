# The Biomechanics of Better Sleep

In the world of sleep optimization, the most effective solutions are often the simplest. At **HLK**, we've spent over 20 years perfecting medical-grade adhesive technology to solve one of the most common sleep disruptors: mouth breathing.

## Why Nasal Breathing is the Key to Recovery
Nasal breathing isn't just a habit??t's a biological necessity for high performance. When you breathe through your nose, your body:
- **Produces Nitric Oxide**: A natural vasodilator that improves oxygen uptake.
- **Filters and Warms Air**: Protecting your lungs and throat from irritation.
- **Regulates Sleep Cycles**: Reducing micro-arousals and snoring.
